## üá¶üá∫ Australian Government Grants & Funding

### 1. R&D Tax Incentive (RDTI) ‚≠ê MOST VALUABLE

**What:** 43.5% refundable tax offset on eligible R&D expenses

| Your Spend | Refund You Get |
|------------|----------------|
| $100,000 | $43,500 |
| $200,000 | $87,000 |
| $500,000 | $217,500 |

**Eligibility:**
- Australian company with turnover < $20M
- Conducting eligible R&D activities in Australia
- Anchor's AI/ML development qualifies!

**How to Apply:**
1. Register with AusIndustry BEFORE financial year ends
2. Document R&D activities and expenses
3. Lodge application with tax return
4. Receive cash refund within 60 days

**Timeline:** Apply after financial year (July-October)

**Link:** [business.gov.au/rdti](https://business.gov.au/grants-and-programs/research-and-development-tax-incentive)

---

### 2. Export Market Development Grant (EMDG)

**What:** Up to $770,000 over 8 years for export marketing

| Year | Maximum Grant |
|------|---------------|
| Year 1-2 | $40,000/year |
| Year 3-4 | $80,000/year |
| Year 5-8 | $150,000/year |

**Eligible Expenses:**
- Overseas marketing & advertising
- Trade shows & conferences
- International travel for sales
- Website localization

**How to Apply:**
1. Register on Austrade portal
2. Track eligible export expenses
3. Apply after spending occurs
4. Receive reimbursement (50% of costs)

**Link:** [austrade.gov.au/emdg](https://www.austrade.gov.au/australian/export/export-grants/emdg)
